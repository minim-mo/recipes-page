Filet Mignon with Rich Balsamic Glaze

Balsamic Glaze for Steak Ingredients These are the ingredients you'll need to add to your grocery list: Steaks: You'll need two filet mignon steaks for this restaurant-worthy recipe. Seasonings: This filet mignon is simply seasoned with salt and pepper. Balsamic vinegar: Of course, you'll need balsamic vinegar. It's the star of the show! Wine: Choose a dry red wine, such as cabernet sauvignon.

  • 2 (4 ounce) filet mignon steaks
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper, or to taste
  • salt to taste
  • ¼ cup balsamic vinegar
  • ¼ cup dry red wine
  1. Step 1: Gather ingredients.
  2. Step 2: Sprinkle both sides of steaks with pepper and salt.
  3. Step 3: Heat a nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Sear steaks in the hot pan until browned, about 1 minute per side.
  4. Step 4: Reduce heat to medium-low, and pour in balsamic vinegar and red wine. Cover and cook for 4 minutes. Flip steaks and baste with sauce; cover and cook for 4 minutes longer.
  5. Step 5: Remove steaks to two warmed plates. Spoon a tablespoon of glaze over each steak and serve immediately.
